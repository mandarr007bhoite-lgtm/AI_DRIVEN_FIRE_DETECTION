Aim: To develop an AI-driven fire detection and suppression system.

Objectives:

  • Alert users instantly with sound & voice notifications.
  • Maintain a detection history log.
  • Prevent accidents before they escalate.

Scope:

This system can be deployed in homes, industries, offices, and public spaces for fire safety.
